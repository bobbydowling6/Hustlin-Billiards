Events

At Hustlin Billiards, our professional event planners will help make your next event unforgettable! We offer private rooms, pool tables, open bars, pre-paid drink tickets, and custom appetizer buffets. No matter what kind of party you’re looking to put on, we’ll make it happen. Whether it's pre-game and post-game parties, office shindigs, birthdays, bachelor and bachelorette parties, pool tournaments, wedding receptions, you name it. We offer a great event party package that you can rent out the entire bar for your exciting party. Our rates start as low as $50 an hour with free pool tables to play with friends, an appetizers buffet, and open bar for beer only. For our cocktail menu, we will give a 15% discount as our event specials. Just fill out our contact form and we'll contact you shortly to reserve a private room or even the whole bar for your special occasion.
