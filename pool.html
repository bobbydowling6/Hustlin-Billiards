Want to improve your game? Practice makes perfect! We have 15 Brunswick pool tables waiting for you. We are a Billiards bar after all and that's what we want our customers to say when they have fun at our bar.Hustlin Billiards is the best place around to practice your game at our discount hourly rates. You can play pool for the price of $10 an hour. We also host our very own billiards tournament at the end of each month,There is no entry fee to enter, you just have to reserve a spot in advance. You will play against top pool players in town for bragging rights and winner will receive top prize of $1000. The types of pool games we play in our prestige tournament incude: 8 ball, 9 ball, Cut Throat, and Rotation. All of our tournaments are fun, co-ed, and also a great way to meet other pool players! You can join our monthly tournament as an individual or even join as a group with your friends and play as a team, we do host team tournaments as well. For more info on how to play those types of pool games and what the rules are, click here: Learn How to Play Pool
