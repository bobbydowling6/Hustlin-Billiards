Welcome to Hustlin Billiards! We are Chicagoland's best and finest billiards bar around. We offer 15 Brunswick pool tables, ping pong tables, darts, and foosball. We host a variety of co-ed pool tournaments that are scheduled in the end of every month. Hustlin Billiards has an extensive cocktail menu that uses the freshest ingredients to make the best cocktails around. We also have a beer and a specialty appetizers menu. Hustlin Billiards is open every day of the year from 4PM to 12AM Sunday-Thursday and 5PM to 4AM Friday-Saturday. We even take reservations and can accommodate corporate parties, private parties and group events up to 500 people. Just fill out our contact form and we'll contact you shortly to reserve a private room or even the whole bar for your special occasion.
